About 961Summits

961Summits is your gateway to exploring Lebanon's natural beauty. From the highest peaks to the lushest valleys, we aim to connect adventurers with the breathtaking landscapes of Lebanon.

Our mission is to inspire people to explore the outdoors, embrace adventure, and create unforgettable memories. Whether you're a seasoned hiker or a beginner, we provide the resources, guidance, and support you need to embark on your journey.

Our Vision

We envision a world where people are deeply connected to nature, fostering a sense of responsibility to protect and preserve the environment for future generations.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced trail guides with in-depth knowledge of Lebanon's landscapes.
  • Customizable adventure packages tailored to your preferences.
  • High-quality equipment rentals for a safe and enjoyable experience.
  • A commitment to sustainability and eco-friendly practices.
